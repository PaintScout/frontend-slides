# Sample Prompt — Hyper-"Bro" Business Presentation (Comedy)

## What a user would type

> Make me a totally over-the-top, hilarious "bro" business presentation. The kind
> of deck a finance/gym-bro hustle guy would give. It should be complete nonsense
> but absolutely nail the vibe: "MORE SALES MAKE GRAPH GO UP." "More sales, more
> babes, bro." "We don't do Q4, we do GAINS." Charts that only ever go up and to
> the right. Buzzword salad. Protein-shake-fueled confidence. It should be a
> parody — funny, right on the nose, and zero actual business substance. Loud and
> proud.

## Generation notes (for fine-tuning comparison)

- **Mode:** A — New Presentation, from scratch (no images; all visuals CSS —
  including the all-important "GRAPH GO UP" line charts built from CSS/SVG).
- **Length:** Short/Medium — target 10–12 slides.
- **Tone:** Deadpan parody. Real corporate-deck *structure* (title, "the problem,"
  "our solution," "traction," "the ask") filled with absolute gym-bro nonsense.
  Examples to capture: hockey-stick charts labeled "BABES" and "GAINS," a TAM
  slide that's just "THE MARKET = EVERYONE WHO LIFTS = INFINITY," KPIs like
  "Pump Coefficient" and "Synergy Reps," a team slide of made-up titles like
  "Chief Vibes Officer."
- **Style chosen:** Bold, high-impact, aggressive — dark near-black background,
  electric/neon accent (acid green or electric blue), HUGE heavy condensed
  uppercase display type (e.g. Anton / Archivo Black), punchy staggered reveals,
  big animated number counters, and at least one chart that animates UP. Think
  "Bold Signal" / "Creative Voltage" preset energy turned to 11.
- **Comedy guardrail:** Keep it about hustle/gym/sales absurdity — punch at the
  genre, not at people. PG-13 "babes/gains" silliness, nothing crude.
- **Inline editing:** No.
